The problem with optimization

Most approaches to AI visibility are search optimization with a new name. They optimize content for mention. That's not how selection works.

Structure, not content

We're building the structural layer that influences how AI systems resolve uncertainty. Not through more content or better keywords. Through architecture.

How information relates to other information matters as much as the information itself. We build those relationships so probability collapses in your direction.
