AI Search Doesn't Rank Your Brand.
It Selects It. Or Doesn't.

By J. Barbush  ·  Founder, Stage30

Steve means well. He has been your SEO guy for three years. He brings bagels. He has a slide that shows your brand ranking number four in ChatGPT. He has circled it in orange. He is proud of it.

Steve is not wrong that the number exists. He is wrong about what it means. AI search does not assign positions that hold. It selects, freshly, every single time someone asks. The orange circle is a screenshot of one moment in a system that had already moved on by the time he hit print.
