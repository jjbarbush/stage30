We're building something different for how brands show up in AI. Not better SEO. Not better content. A structural approach to how AI systems resolve uncertainty and select answers.

What we're working on

AI doesn't work like search engines. Search engines rank pages. AI systems collapse probability into answers. That's a fundamentally different mechanism, and it requires a fundamentally different approach.

We're developing the structural layer that influences how AI systems resolve ambiguity. Not through keywords or citations or content volume. Through architecture.

How information relates to other information matters as much as the information itself. We're building the framework that makes those relationships explicit.

Where we are now

Early testing with a handful of clients. Documenting what works, what doesn't, and why. We're not launching until we can show this works reliably.
